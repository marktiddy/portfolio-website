My Work Contact

I am a full-stack developer from Colchester, Essex having recently achieved the CareerFoundry Web Development certification. I have experience with a wide-range of technologies with more in-depth knowledge of JavaScript, React and Wordpress alongside being familiar with Github for version control and both SQL and noSQL databases. Coding enables me to express my passion for creativity and problem solving.

My professional background is in youth work and youth work training which I have worked in for over 15 years, providing me with excellent communication, networking and presentation skills with plenty of opportunities to innovate and create new projects.

Alongside my full-time job I have been involved in working with charities to develop websites for their projects and have provided follow-up training in order to enable them to update their own websites.
